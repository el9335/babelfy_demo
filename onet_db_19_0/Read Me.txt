O*NET 19.0 Database
July 2014 Release
-----------------

The current release of the O*NET database is available online at:

http://www.onetcenter.org/database.html

For information on future releases of the O*NET database, visit
the O*NET Development Database page at:

http://www.onetcenter.org/dev_db.html


New in 19.0
-----------

126 occupations were comprehensively updated, bringing the total to
940, in this fifteenth major update from the O*NET Data Collection
Program. Highlights of this update include:

* Addition and update of Task Statement ratings (importance,
  relevance, and frequency) for 126 occupations
* Addition and update of Abilities, Skills, Work Activities,
  Knowledge, and Work Context data for 126 occupations
* Addition and update of Training and Work Experience and Education
  data for 126 occupations
* Addition and update of Work Styles data for 126 occupations
* Addition and update of Job Zones data for 126 occupations
* Addition and update of Detailed Work Activities (DWAs) and
  linkages for 126 occupations
